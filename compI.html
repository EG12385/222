Company Information



The following steps are performed only once after system installation.


Updating your company information

1. On the Company info panel the following can be specified:

  • Company Name - The conventional form of your companyâ€™s name.
  • Base Currency - Internal currency is predefined for each new PortaBilling environment, which is also the currency used when creating 'Cost / Revenue Reports'. For customers and vendors, different currencies can be used.
  • Time Zone - Currently Africa/Johannesburg, this is the time zone in which global reports will be generated from your system envirionment.
  • Reconcilliation Period - How frequently the global reports will be generated

2. Address information is concerned with the legal information, the following can be updated:

  • "From" email for outgoing communication send from your billing environment - An email address that will apear on all outgoing notifications send from the system to your customers, or users, e.g. statistics, invoices, credit warnings etc.
  • Contact info for invoices - Contact details about your comapny, used on reports as the primary point of contact for your vendors and customers. Same information that appears on your company letterhead and business cards.
  • Contact email - Email that your customers can use to contact you, this email can also be added on a invoice template and appear on issued invoices.

3. Use Legal adrress for invoices

On the Invoicing and taxation panel the following information should be filled in:

  • Invoice number sequence - Sequencing level for invoice numbers
    • Individual for environment - Sequential invoice numbering throughout the environment
    • Individual for reseller - Sequential invoice numbering throughout the environment for direct customers, distributors and resellers. Resellers will have their own sequential numbering for all their customers.
    • Individual for customer - Every customer will have their own sequential numbering, (direct customers, subcustomer, distributer and reseller).


4. Click Save.
