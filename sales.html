Below describes the sales functionalities used to provide service to your customers, in support of certain paramaters and billing preferences:



Customer Classes

A customer class provides the ability to define the business model grouping parameters in a centralized manner, applying those parameters to many customers simultaneously.

The following functions and options are provided:

  • Creating your customer class
  • Finance General Information
  • Configure the Statistics parameters:
    • Generate statistics
    • The option to send statistics via mail
  • Customer status changes parameters:
    • Suspend on insufficient funds for subscriptions
  • Finances Invoicing:
    • Use regular invoices
    • Invoice template
    • Generate invoice pdf
    • Send invoices via email
    • Configure Payment terms, Payment collection and Action upon non-payment parameters
  • Taxation
    • Fixed VAT Rate
    • Custom taxation
    • Taxes included into rates

Creating Resellers

The reseller entity you are about to create will define your relationship with a business partner who is reselling your services under his own name and allow them further independent service management (e.g. creating their own customers and accounts).

Similar to that of customer creation, yet the following additional actions will be required:

  • Web self-care
  • Credit Limit
  • Reseller 'Cost' rate to be paid to you
  • Voice product/s managed by the customer to assign to their customers

Fraud Protection Configuration

The Fraud protection functionality for customers who use services within other business models is configured in two steps:

1. Fraud protection configuration in individual products

2. Configuration at the account level


Creating a geo / risk profile, the following can be done:

  • Create a geo / risk profile
  • Add geo / risk profile to the product
  • Fraud protection configuration on customer sites
